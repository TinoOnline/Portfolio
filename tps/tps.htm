Project Description

Technological Plumbing Solutions(TPS) is a township based social startup that provides clients an affordable, easy and effective IOT solution to solve water leaks and sewer blockages. The IOT solution has a public reporting App, which allows the general public to easily report water leaks and sewage blockages faster by just taking a picture of the problem and sending it. The App uses your location to pin the address of where the problem is reported and send that to your municipal offices.
