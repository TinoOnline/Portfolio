Project Description

Change Ready is an assessment based platform that can be used to evaluate the change readiness of users. The platform can be used by; Organisations with large departments, small departments, even individuals can take assessments to evaluate themselves. The platform offers graphed result reports that can then be converted to different formats for different uses.
