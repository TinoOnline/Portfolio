Project Description

AMO Wallet is an online application that helps you place orders and make payments from the convenience of your smartphone, a perfect alternative for cash and cards. AMO Wallet helps you order and pay at Schools, Events, Hotels and more. You don’t have to access your banking apps in public areas and risk your safety and security.
