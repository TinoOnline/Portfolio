Project Description

Nukleus is a cloud-based file & document storage application for both people and small to medium sized organisations to store and retrieve documents and other important information. Nukleus helps store your information on the cloud and access it whenever you need it, wherever you are.It makes it easier to find all your records in seconds or even share documents or files with colleagues.
